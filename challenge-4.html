HOME

Web development involves building and maintaining websites or web applications. It includes front-end development (what users see) using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and back-end development (server-side logic) using languages like Python, PHP, or Node.js. Developers may also work with databases, APIs, and version control systems like Git. Web development ensures a site is functional, responsive, and user-friendly. It ranges from simple static pages to complex, dynamic web applications.
Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications that run on the internet. It involves both front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) development to ensure smooth functionality and user interaction. From simple static pages to complex dynamic platforms, web development brings ideas to life online. It combines coding, design, and problem-solving to create responsive, secure, and user-friendly digital experiences. As technology evolves, web development continues to play a crucial role in shaping how we interact, shop, learn, and do business in the digital world.
