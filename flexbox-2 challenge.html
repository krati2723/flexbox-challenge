Mahakal Jyotirlinga

Mahakal Jyotirlinga, located in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, is one of the twelve sacred Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. It is the only Jyotirlinga where Lord Shiva is worshipped as *Mahakaal*, the Lord of Time and Death. The temple is renowned for its Bhasma Aarti, a unique ritual performed with sacred ash.

Kedarnath Jyotirlinga

Kedarnath is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand. It is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a key site of the Char Dham Yatra. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, the temple holds deep spiritual significance and attracts thousands of pilgrims each year.

Somnath Jyotirlinga

Somnath Temple, located in Gujarat, is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and holds great religious and historical significance. It is believed to be the first Jyotirlinga where Lord Shiva manifested. The temple has been rebuilt several times, symbolizing resilience and devotion.
